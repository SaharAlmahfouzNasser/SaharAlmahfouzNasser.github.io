Intro

Hi,I am a Ph.D. scholar at the IIT-B. I am interested in solving health care problems using deep learning. The core topic of my Ph.D. is image-guided surgery. We are developing deep learning-based registration techniques for fusing different imaging modalities. By the way, check out my awesome work.

I did many intersting projects with both academia and industry such as Qualcomm, and Whirlpool.

About

I am the winner of Qualcomm prestigious fellowship 2021-2022 for the proposal MRI-Ultrasound Fusion in Prostate Diagnosis and Surgery. It was pretty competitive, but our proposal made it through multiple rounds and evaluations.

I am a reviewer of Papers for National and International Conferences such as the IEEE International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging and the National Conference on Communications.

I was one of the organizers and Presenters at Koita Center for Digital Health KCDH Workshop.

I am an TA of many courses such as an introduction to machine learning, digital image processing, digital signal processing, multimedia, and microprocessors.

I am a team leader of many projects at MeDAL.

I won prestigious certificates such as:

• Certificate of Participation in the 3rd Industrial Day organised by the Wadhwani Research Center | IIT-B [2019]

• Honour Code Certificate for Technical - Communication for Scientists and Engineers | IIT-B [2017]

• Five times winner of the Certificate of Martyr Bassel Alassad for Study Superiority [2011 - 2015].
